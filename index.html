Classic cheesecake Recipe

Look no further for o creamy ond ultra smooth classic cheesecake Poired with o buttery graham crocker Crust. no one can deny its simple decadence. For the best results. bake in a water bath.

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crust

1 and 1/2 cups(150g) graham Cracker crumbs (about 10 full sheet graham Cracker)
5 Tablespoons(70g) unsalted butter, melted
1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar

Cheesecake

four 8-ounce blocks(940g) full-fat cream cheese, softened to room temperature
1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
1 cups(240g) full-fat sour cream at room temperature
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
2 teaspoon fresh lemon juice (optional,but recommended)
3 large egg, at room temperature
topping suggestions: salted caramel, lemon curd, strawberry topping, chocolate ganache, red wine chocolate ganache, fresh fruit, whipped cream, or raspberry sauce (recipe in notes)

Instructions

Adjust the oven rack to the lower-middle position and preheat oven to 3SOOF (1770C).
